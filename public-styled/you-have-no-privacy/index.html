Why This Matters

Important note This demo shows approximate location, not exact street-level location.
Classroom prompt Imagine what a site can do when this is combined with accounts, cookies, and long-term tracking.
Tip for class demos: try this once normally, then try again with a VPN, a different browser, or a phone on mobile data.
