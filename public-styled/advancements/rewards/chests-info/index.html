How to Get Chests

This page explains exactly how chests are awarded in the Troop 248 rewards system.

← Back to Rewards Open Chests View Badges

Activity Rewards

Meetings

Every full meeting awards:

  • 1 Lucky Chest
  • 1 Gold Chest

Service Projects

Every service project awards:

  • 1 Lucky Chest per hour
  • 1 Gold Chest per hour

Campouts

Every campout awards:

  • 1 Lucky Chest per half day

Example: Arrive Friday, all day Saturday, depart Sunday = 4 Lucky Chests

Merit Badge Rewards

Eagle-Required Merit Badges

Every completed Eagle-required merit badge awards:

  • 5 Lucky Chests
  • 5 Gold Chests

Elective Merit Badges

Every completed elective merit badge awards:

  • 1 Lucky Chest
  • 10 Gold Chests

Badge Progress

These rewards are granted when the merit badge is fully completed.

Rank Up Rewards

Scout through 1st Class

Every rank up from Scout through 1st Class awards:

  • 1 Level Up Chest
  • 5 Lucky Chests

Star and Life

Ranking up to Star and Life award:

  • 1 Level Up Chest
  • 10 Lucky Chests
  • 10 Gold Chests

Eagle Rank

Ranking up to Eagle awards:

  • 5 Level Up Chests
  • 25 Lucky Chests
  • 25 Gold Chests

Special Reward

If a scout had already completed Scout rank or higher as of March 17, 2026, they are eligible for:

  • 1 Level Up Chest + 5 Lucky Chests if Scout-1st Class
  • 1 Level Up Chest + 10 Lucky Chests + 10 Gold Chests if Star or Life
  • 5 Level Up Chests + 25 Lucky Chests + 25 Gold Chests if Eagle

More Information

For more details about what chests do after you earn them, return to the Rewards page or open the Chests and Badges sections directly.
