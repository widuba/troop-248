Core Model

Lucky Chest flow is fixed: start at Level 1, roll 5 independent level-up checks at 45.73% each, cap at Level 6, choose a rarity uniformly from the allowed rarities at that final level, choose one badge uniformly from the 5 badges in that rarity, then roll points uniformly from that level-and-rarity range.

Fixed

Final level odds, rarity odds, badge odds, point ranges, and long-run expected values are all fixed by the code.

Not Player-Controlled

There is no better or worse way to open a Lucky Chest. Opening behavior does not change the odds.

Assumption Used Here

This sheet ignores rank restrictions and assumes all 25 badges are available at all times.
